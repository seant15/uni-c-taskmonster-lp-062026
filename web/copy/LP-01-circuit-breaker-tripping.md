**H2:** What a Helper can walk through on video — and when to call a pro

| Tag | Topic |
| --- | --- |
| GREEN — Safe to explore on video | Tripped breaker resets, overloaded circuit checks, labeling breakers, GFCI/AFCI nuisance trips, "what is this breaker for?" |
| AMBER — Proceed with caution | Replacing a breaker (if you're experienced), multi-wire branch circuit questions, older panel identification |
| RED — Stop and hire a licensed electrician | Burning smell, scorched breakers, main panel work, aluminum wiring, any shock or spark, work that requires a permit |

**Honest line:** Task Monsters connects you with a Subject Matter Expert for guidance on a live video call. We are not a replacement for a licensed electrician when code, permits, or safety require one.

## Section 9 — FAQ (tm-faq)

**Is this a replacement for an electrician?**  
No — and your Helper will tell you when you need one. Many calls end with a clear DIY fix. Some end with "call a licensed electrician Monday morning." Both outcomes are worth $9.99.

**How is this different from JustAnswer or DIY Solve?**  
No monthly subscription. No $30–50 session fee. Live video of your actual setup with a Subject Matter Expert — not text chat with a stranger.

**Is the fit check part of the app?**  
No. The quiz is a free self-check on this web page only. The app is where you choose your trade, topic, and Helper to get rescued on live video.

**What if the breaker keeps tripping after the call?**  
Your Helper will tell you the next step — whether that's a part to buy, a circuit to relocate, or a pro to book.

**Do I pay if I don't use it?**  
No. Download the app free. You pay $9.99 only when you start a call.

**Is it safe to show my breaker panel on video?**  
Yes — you're showing what any electrician would look at first. Don't touch anything you're unsure about; that's what the call is for.
