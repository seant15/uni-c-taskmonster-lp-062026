## $9.99 scope honesty (Joseph 2026-06-29)

Joseph edited LPs: prior copy **over-promised** what callers get for $9.99.

Marketing must promise:

- One live video session with a Subject Matter Expert who sees **your** situation.
- Guidance, diagnosis, what to try next, when to call a licensed pro.
- Negotiation coaching / quote interpretation where applicable.

Marketing must NOT promise:

- Guaranteed fix, guaranteed savings amount (unless verified case study with approval).
- On-site work, parts included, or follow-up visits in the fee.
- Instant connection every time (use goal ~60 sec, up to 10 min).
- Brand-specific authorized repair or install.
